Balinese cuisine is a cuisine tradition of Balinese people from the volcanic island of Bali. Using a variety of spices, blended with the fresh vegetables, meat and fish. Mie Goreng (fried noodles) or Nasi Goreng (fried rice) are probably the most famous street food options in Bali. Other favourites include fried chicken sate with peanut sauce, Nasi Jinggo (a ready-to-eat rice dish very popular in street markets in Bali), Nasi Campur, etc.
